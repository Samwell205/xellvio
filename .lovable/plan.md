I’ll guide you through the exact Lovable steps — no code changes are needed.

1. In your Lovable project, look at the top/side navigation and open **Cloud**.
2. Inside **Cloud**, click **Emails**.
3. Find the email sender domain, likely **notify.xellvio.com**.
4. Click **Verify Domain** or **Manage Domains**, then **Verify Domain**.
5. Wait for the status to change to **Active**.
6. After it becomes Active, go back to your app’s Team page and click **Resend email** for the invited person.

If you are on mobile:
1. Switch to **Chat mode** if needed.
2. Tap the **three dots (...)** in the bottom-right.
3. Tap **Cloud**.
4. Open the **Emails** tab.
5. Tap **Verify Domain** for **notify.xellvio.com**.
